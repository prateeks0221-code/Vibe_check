### 1. Vision & Philosophy
The status quo is broken. ### 2. The Core MVP Features

#### 2.1 The Mirror (Real-Time Raw Feed)
*   **Description:** High-definition, low-latency streaming directly from the venue's CCTV/Media MTX pipeline to the user's smartphone.
*   **Implementation:** Multi-angle toggles. Users can swipe between the bar, the entrance, and the center of the dance floor.
*   **The "Revolutionary" Edge:** Zero filters. Zero delays. Complete transparency. Users see exactly who is there, what they are wearing, and how they are moving.

#### 2.2 Occupancy & Waitlist Prediction
*   **Occupancy %:** Live percentage of venue capacity.
*   **Waitlist Prediction:** AI analyzes the entry/exit flow to predict exactly how many minutes a user will stand in line. 
*   **FOMO Trigger:** Push notifications when a "hidden gem" venue suddenly hits 90% capacity.

#### 2.3 Vibe Archetypes (Demographic Intelligence)
*   **Feature:** Instead of boring charts, the AI categorizes the crowd into "Archetypes" based on age, style, and gender split.
*   **Examples:**
    *   *Gen-Z Techies* (Predominantly 21-25, casual/streetwear)
    *   *Late-Night Hedonists* (High energy, 2 a.m. peak, eclectic style)
    *   *30+ Sophisticates* (High-end cocktails, lounge attire, 30-40 age bracket)
*   **Gender Ratio Alerts:** Real-time alerts like "Balanced Vibe Detected" or "High Female Density (65%)" to drive immediate traffic.

#### 2.4 Energy Level & Dance Floor Activity
*   **Metric:** A proprietary **Energy Score (0-100)**.
*   **Logic:** AI analyzes movement velocity, density of the dance floor, and light/flash patterns.
*   **Value:** Users can distinguish between a "dead" club and a "riot" in real-time.

#### 2.5 Vibe Zones
*   **Feature:** Spatial mapping of the venue into distinct AI-defined zones.
*   **Categories:**
    *   **Dark & Moody:** Corner booths, low movement, intimate lighting.
    *   **High Energy:** Center stage, maximum Energy Score.
    *   **Socialite Hub:** The main bar area, high interaction frequency.
*   **User Action:** Users can "tap" a zone on their phone to see the specific stream for that area.

### 3. User Experience & FOMO Engine
*   **Global Map:** A network of 100+ cafes/bars/clubs integrated into a seamless "Vibe Grid." Venues glow brighter on the map as their Energy Score increases, creating a real-time heat map of human desire.
*   **The "Pulse" Feed:** A TikTok-style vertical scroll of the most "active" Mirrors currently streaming. AI auto-selects the most chaotic or high-energy angles to hook the user.
*   **The VIP Override:** A feature for 0.0001% users to "claim" a vibe, signaling to others where the elite are congregating.
*   **Alert System:** "The Vibe is peaking at *Club X*. 70% Gender Balance. Energy Level 92. 5-minute wait. Go now or miss the night."
